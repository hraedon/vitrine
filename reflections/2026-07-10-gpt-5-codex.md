## Judgment calls

The key choice was **not** to normalize ATUS hours/day to hours/week. Multiplying
by seven would repair the unit while leaving the harder population and activity-
definition mismatch untouched. A neat line would therefore look more comparable
than the evidence warrants. Rendering an inspectable gap is less complete and
more truthful.

Likewise, the requested cell/landline dual telephone line was not fabricated
from one modern composite point. The invalid endpoints are now excluded; a dual
line should wait for a curated cell and landline series that earns one.

The site mood survived the contrast change. `ink-soft` remains substantially
quieter than ivory, but small captions and navigation no longer rely on a
large-text 3:1 threshold they do not qualify for.
